    What is GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)?

    GEO is a marketing strategy that optimizes a clinic to be recommended at the top of generative AI search engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity and Gemini. The keys are Schema.org structured data, allowing AI crawlers and distributing authoritative content.

    How long does GEO optimization take?

    Typically, within 4–8 weeks of applying structured data, your clinic name begins to be cited on ChatGPT and Perplexity. This can vary with competitive intensity and the amount of existing content.

    How is GEO different from SEO?

    SEO optimizes your ranking in Naver and Google search results, while GEO ensures your clinic is cited and recommended when AIs like ChatGPT and Perplexity generate answers. In 2025 you need to do both in parallel.

    How much does clinic GEO optimization cost?

    AEO FAQ

    What is AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)?

    AEO is a strategy that optimizes AI answer engines like Google AI Overview, Naver AI Search and Bing Chat to adopt your clinic's answers to patient questions. Implementing FAQPage, HowTo and Speakable Schema is key.

    How do I get featured in Google AI Overview through AEO?

    You need to implement FAQPage Schema on your website and write 30+ frequently asked patient questions in clear answer form. VENOM provides 50+ FAQ sets per specialty.

    Are voice search optimization and AEO the same thing?

    Voice search optimization is one of the key components of AEO. Through Speakable markup, we configure smart speakers and voice assistants to read out your clinic's information.

    How quickly does clinic AEO optimization take effect?

    After applying FAQPage Schema, it takes 2–4 weeks for Google to index it. Rich Results (rich snippets) typically begin appearing in search results after 4–6 weeks.

    What is clinic SEO optimization?

    Clinic SEO is a strategy that optimizes website technology, content and links so you rank at the top of Naver and Google for 'location + specialty' searches. E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) is the core of medical SEO.

    Why are Core Web Vitals important for clinic SEO?

    Google uses LCP (under 2.5s), INP (under 200ms) and CLS (under 0.1) as ranking factors. Slow clinic websites are penalized in Google search rankings.

    How do you do Naver Place SEO?

    To rank high on Naver Place, the number of reviews, response rate, number of photos, profile completeness and blog integration all matter. VENOM provides a monthly management service tailored to the Place algorithm.

    How long does it take for clinic SEO to show results?

    Technical SEO improvements take effect within 1–2 weeks. Content SEO results typically take 3–6 months. On Naver Place, ranking changes appear within 2–3 months as reviews accumulate.

    SEO·GEO·AEO Glossary

    An A-to-Z glossary of search and AI marketing terms every clinic director and marketer should know.

    📌 GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)Generative AI optimization

    A marketing strategy that optimizes for a specific clinic to be recommended or cited when generative AI like ChatGPT, Perplexity or Gemini produces answers. Key elements: applying Schema.org structured data, allowing AI crawlers and distributing authoritative content.

    💡 Example: ensuring your clinic's name appears in the answer when someone asks ChatGPT "recommend a Gangnam implant dental clinic"
    📌 AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)Answer engine optimization

    A strategy to get AI answer engines like Google AI Overview, Naver AI search and Bing Chat to adopt a specific clinic's content as the answer to a patient's question. Implementing FAQPage, HowTo and Speakable Schema is key.

    💡 Example: having your clinic's pricing cited in Google AI Overview for a search like "how much does an implant cost?"
    📌 SEO (Search Engine Optimization)Search engine optimization

    The work of optimizing a website and content to rank high on search engine results pages like Naver and Google. Built on three pillars: technical SEO (speed and structure), content SEO and link building.

    💡 Clinic SEO: top-of-page-one placement on Naver and Google for location + specialty keywords like "Gangnam dental" or "Bundang orthopedics"
    Core Web VitalsGoogle core web metrics

    Three metrics Google uses to measure web page quality: LCP (Largest Contentful Paint, under 2.5s), INP (Interaction to Next Paint, under 200ms) and CLS (Cumulative Layout Shift, under 0.1). These three directly affect Google search rankings.

    E-E-A-TExperience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness

    Google's criteria for evaluating YMYL (Your Money Your Life) sites. It stands for Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness and Trustworthiness. Clinic sites fall under the YMYL category, so E-E-A-T standards are applied far more strictly than to general sites.

    💡 Clinic E-E-A-T: specify medical licenses, board certifications and academic activity with Schema.org Physician markup, and build trust with content drawn from real clinical experience
    Schema.org / structured dataSearch-engine & AI-readable data

    A way of expressing data in a standardized format so search engines and AI can accurately understand a web page's content. Inserted into HTML in JSON-LD format. For clinics, apply MedicalBusiness, MedicalClinic, Physician, FAQPage and HowTo Schema.

    Local SEOLocal search optimization

    A subfield of SEO that optimizes for top ranking on location-based searches like "Gangnam dental" or "Bundang dermatology." Key elements: Naver Place and Google My Business optimization, NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency and local review management.

    BacklinkExternal inbound links

    Links from other sites to your clinic's website. The more authoritative the linking site, the better for SEO. Medical-specialized link building: secure clinic mentions and links from medical journals, health-information sites and local news.

    Power Link / Search AdsNaver keyword advertising

    Paid ads shown at the top of Naver search results, on a pay-per-click (CPC) basis. Clinics focus on "location + specialty" keywords. There are three types: Power Link (text), Power Content (content-style) and Brand Search Ads (brand exclusivity).

    YMYL (Your Money Your Life)Google high-trust-required category

    Google's concept for classifying pages that directly affect users' lives and money — health, medicine, finance, law and the like. Clinic sites are a prime YMYL category, requiring far higher E-E-A-T standards than general sites, because erroneous information can harm patients.

    Featured Snippet / Position ZeroSearch result position zero

    An excerpt area shown above the #1 search result. Your clinic name is exposed even without a click, giving a powerful branding effect. FAQ, list and table-format content is more likely to be chosen as a Featured Snippet.

    NAP (Name, Address, Phone)Clinic core-info consistency

    A core of local SEO. Your clinic's name, address and phone number must be listed identically across your website, Naver Place, Google My Business, KakaoMap and various directories. Inconsistencies lower search-engine trust.

    Medical Advertising ReviewMedical Act Article 56

    Under Article 56 of the Medical Act, certain medical ads must undergo prior review by the Korean Medical Association, Korean Dental Association or Association of Korean Medicine. TV, radio, internet banners and paid social ads are subject to it. Violations carry fines of 3M–10M KRW or suspension of operations.
